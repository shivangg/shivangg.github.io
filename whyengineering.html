WHY ENGINEERING

Ever since childhood, numbers and mechanisms inside machines(even toys) always intrigued me and i always thought how every component worked so smoothly with each other to create such a beautiful system to do the interesting actions. I always wanted to design such interesting practical systems.I questioned natural phenomenon(from simple rainfall and rainbow to mighty Aurora Borealis) at every step. Only science had the potential to answer these questions,thus, I naturally developed a liking for them. As engineering would let me to tackle the problems of consumers directly, I chose it as my only career option.

PS. I was never great at memorizing loads of stuff, thus engineering became my favorite stream.
